Major Improvements ahead for the Cross City Project

With the Cross City Project underway and the closure of the Haysbridge Line
coming soon, buses will replace trains from the next available terminus to Thornsbury & Welshpool stations.

Each respective lines/groups will terminate at the following stations (applies to Suburban and Regional trains):

Line: Terminus
Lyndale Tarrawallee
Kathryn Tarrawallee
Tunbridge Wells Tarrawallee
Murray Banbury
Everdeen East Fritton
Fairmead Shipton
Tallentyne Cannock
Wynthorpe Leighton
Wellington Spearwood
Avandale Carlisle
Moorinallea Belmont Park
Tottenham (Regional Exp.) Myrtle Point
Denton (Regional Exp.) Glendurrie
Wytcombe (Regional Exp.) Myrtle Point
Queensborough (Regional Exp.) Carlisle
Harold Point (Regional Exp.) Tallentyne

 

Trains to Thornsbury, Welshpool & All city loop stations will not occur as major
improvements are happening at these stations.

Buses will replace trains from November 15th and works commence from the 16th
until further advised. When works occur, please find alternative travel plans
if these replacement buses do not cater to your needs.

Trains to Thornsbury will resume as fast as possible when all
the necessary trackwork has been completed.

Trains to Welshpool will significantly suffer during these works, as a whole
station revamp must occur for the Cross City Project, regional trains will resume
as fast as possible once all the important works have been made.

Customers for Haysbridge are advised no more rail services will be happening
from November 8th, after that, please travel by bus or tram. We thank those who
have travelled on the Haysbridge Line over the last 74 years of revenue trains.

As an extended thanks, we will have Z1 (hauled by a locomotive) travel on the
Avandale Line on November 8th through the last scheduled train at 9:55PM. After
10pm, no trains will operate on the line permanently.

Thank you for understanding the upcoming works ahead, and we are looking forward
to making your train services smooth.

Transnet is operated and controlled by the Thornsbury Government Department of Public Transport
